Dashboard

What do we do here

This is a view that enables an automated tracking of workflow and milestone dates for project artifacts and has configurable, automated email and on-screen alerts to notify stakeholders of upcoming due dates.An integrated dashboard to quickly access a range of project management artifacts, documents, and functions.
